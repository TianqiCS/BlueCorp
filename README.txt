Presented by The Blu Team: Hope Docking (Writer), Isabella Fuccaro (Artist), Emily Casavant (Musician), Felipe Salas Noguera (Musician), Tianqi Wang (Programmer) and Alden Tan.

Blu corp. is a day based text/adventure game. The game consists of three days and in every day the player needs to visit three creatures and take care of they. The player needs to be careful while doing their labour in the lab. If He/She takes the wrong decision they could kill some beast, or them could kill you. Some of their duties are feeding the animals and clean their cells. Before the interaction with the creatures, the player can submit their report and continue to the next day.

This is a Twine project by Harlowe 2.1.0 from https://twine2.neocities.org and http://twinery.org/
The music in folder mx is done by Felipe Salas Noguera
The sound effects in the folder sounds are done by Emily Casavant
The art in folder imgs is done by Isabella Fuccaro
The mini game minesweeper is done by Tianqi Wang which is available at https://github.com/TianqiCS/MineSweeper-Remastered
